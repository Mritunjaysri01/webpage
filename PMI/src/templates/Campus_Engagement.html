Campus Engagement

Right from the beginning, students from different colleges came and volunteer in PMI to help us serve the community in different ways. One of the areas is CHEP where university students and students who are preparing for a civil service exam helped us in teaching the students through a free tuition program. Volunteers are the backbone to PMI and we strongly believed that by working with the student's education system can be made to penetrate at the grass-root level of our Society.


At the same time, PMI aims to educate and bring awareness to college students with the real-time issues and challenges faced by the weaker/vulnerable section of society. We provide them a chance to explore different slums located in different places of North Delhi to conduct a real-time study and witnessed the real struggle because sometimes evidence base information is better than just having theoretical knowledge. We also wanted to train the students to value and respect humanity and inculcate the importance and the need to serve humanity wherever and whenever required. We provide them internships and certificate which will help them to build their carrier and will serve as additional credit in their resume.


Different ways on how PMI involved in the campus:-


Admission:-

Every year more than thousands of students come from different states to Delhi for taking admission to different programs. PMI opens stalls in various colleges like Miranda House, Amity University, Satyavati, etc. and helped freshers with their admissions. PMI also organize Fresher’s Day and at the same time building relationship with the students to make sure that they are not alone and opened up to be a home for them. You can check on areas where PMI works in partnership with NSS (National Service Scheme) of different colleges both inside and outside Delhi to serve humanity.


Hans Raj College:-

We collaborate with the Hari Adma Society and organized for greenery program where we shared on the need to promote Green Environment.


Hindu College: -

With the NSS team we were able to conduct a year program of collecting clothes and redistributing them to slums.


Ramjas College:-

We were invited for a green program,” Bringing Awareness on the Importance of Tree Plantation” where we shared on The Importance of Planting Trees. PMI band even performed in their college festivals.


KiroriMal College: -

Two medical camps were organized in collaboration with the college NSS for all the construction workers and laborers who were building a stadium for hosting Common Wealth Games. PMI band performed in their college functions as well.


Delhi School of economics: -

Department of Geography program Geo Research Forum invited our founding President to be one of the main speakers where he was able to share on the topic of “Health and Well Being.”


Shri Ram College of Commerce: -

Woman education program in Nandlal slum was conducted in collaboration with the NSS team. We were able to reach out to a lot of women who were given free education classes by students from SRCC.


Satyavati: -

Our founding President was invited to speak on Women Rights in their college program. PMI CHEP kids were also invited for campus tours and were allowed to interact with students on the campus. Several NSS students from Satyavati college volunteer with us and helped in different areas of work for the betterment of society.


Miranda House: -

PMI band used to lead during their college festival.


DaulatRam College: -

We were asked to lead the college festival Marathon with our PMI volunteer. We were also given a chance to share about our NGO (PMI). PMI band also performed.


Amity University:

We were invited to put up a PMI stall in their college program and they also provided us a certificate of participation in the National Level Seminar organized by the University.


Apex Universities (Uttarakhand):

We were invited to share about our NGO (PMI) where the PMI band was also able to perform.
